About Me

Hi, I am Yifei Zhang, currently a student focused on research in the fields of psychology and cognitive science.

I am currently applying computational models and LLMs on decision-making and reasoning.

I took undergraduate courses in psychology at Beijing Normal University and MSc Cognitive and Decision Science at UCL.

Research

My research interests are centered around modeling human decision-making and reasoning processes, particularly in the context of irrational decisions and learning under uncertainty. I focus on Bayesian modeling and reinforcement learning to develop more accurate models of our decision-making processes. With the astonishing LLMs, especially GPT-4o, I am keen to combine behavioral experiments with LLMs to identify key differences and similarities between human and AI.

I am familiar with Python and Matlab in experiment implementation and fitting models and I am experienced in fMRI, EEG and DL and RL algorithms. I am also familiar with R, JavaScript, JASP, Inquisit 4 and C.
